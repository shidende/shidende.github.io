About Me

I am a researcher and software developer specializing in the research-driven design and implementation of accessible learning technologies, with a particular focus on augmented reality, inclusive design, and open-source educational tools for higher education contexts. My work integrates empirical educational research with technical development, emphasizing methodological rigor, user-centered design, and the translation of research findings into functional, reproducible software artifacts. I have experience designing and prototyping AR learning applications and authoring tools, with sustained attention to accessibility-aware interaction design and open research practices. I am currently seeking research, research-oriented software development, or applied R&D opportunities within academia, industry, or interdisciplinary innovation environments.

Professional Profile

An academic technologist with integrated expertise

I am an applied researcher, software engineer, lecturer, and senior systems administrator with a career spanning higher education, research environments, and enterprise-scale IT operations. My work is defined by a consistent goal: to connect robust technical systems with practical educational and institutional value.

My professional foundation was built in infrastructure and systems administration, where I designed and maintained secure hybrid environments, campus networks, identity services, virtualisation platforms, and digital learning systems for large academic communities. This background continues to shape my engineering approach through a strong emphasis on reliability, scalability, and security by design.

My more recent academic work has focused on the design and development of accessible augmented reality authoring tools for non-technical educators. Through this research, I combined Design-Based Research, Agile Scrum, usability evaluation, and inclusive design principles to create practical solutions for technology-enhanced learning.

I therefore bring a rare combination of strengths across infrastructure operations, software development, teaching and laboratory support, and research-led innovation.
